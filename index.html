Targeting students in college and university, and early-career professionals who are still deciding their lifelong goals and pursuits. This tool will help to find the ideal place to relocate to based on several criteria that you select:

  • Cost of living
  • Career
  • Culture
  • Climate
  • Activities and Interests
  • Food
  • Quality of Life

After specifying your criteria and their weightings, we will list your top five locations in the world to relocate to, as well as the cost of relocation. Each location will be graded in each category.

So put together the building blocks to plan your relocation to your dream home!
